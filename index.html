What is diabetes?

There are 3 types of diabetes. Diabetes can occur in adults and even kids.The 3 types of diabtes are:

  • Type 1 Diabetes

  • Type 2 Diabetes

  • Gestational Diabetes

Complications

Diabets can lead to some serious complications when you do not take care of yourself. Some complications are:

  • Hypoglycemia

  • Hyperglycemia

  • Skin Complications

  • Eye Complications

  • Neuropathy

  • Foot Complications

  • DKA (Ketoacidosis) & Ketones

  • Kidney Disease

  • Gastroparesis

  • Mental Health
